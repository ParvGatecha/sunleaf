About Us

Welcome to Lime Exim

We, the Lime Exim are proud to be a trusted manufacturer and exporter of the breakfast cereal, snack, brewery, confectionery, bakery, and biscuit industries both in India and around the world. With over a decade of experience, we offer diverse corn-based products, including corn grits, corn meal, corn brewery grits, broken/cracked/crushed corn, corn flour, and corn flakes. Along with that, we have roasted gram products like desi chickpeas or bengal gram, yellow roasted gram, roasted gram split, and roasted gram whole. We also supply Indian maize to meet specific buyer requirements.

Our plant is equipped with fully automatic machinery using the latest state-of-the-art technology in a very hygienic environment with a capacity of 1300 tons per month. The plant is operated by skilled and experienced staff under the strict supervision of qualified personnel. We have our plant located at ranpar, in the Rajkot district of Gujrat State of India, and have good connectivity with international Airports, Seaport like Mundra, and National highway networks providing smooth and uninterrupted logistical movements.
